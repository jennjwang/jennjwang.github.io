Here's how I started and why I build

For the longest time, I believed in technology’s promise to solve society’s greatest challenges, without realizing that technology is a double-edged sword.

With the history of systemic inequities encoded into our everyday devices, I strive to re-engineer human-centric solutions and leverage the power of technology responsibly for social impact. This mission translates into my work wherever I go, whether that’s building a VR interface for human-robot collaboration at NIST or measuring the efficacy of Covid recovery programs at the RI state government.
